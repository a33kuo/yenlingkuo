Abstract
Humans can plan efficiently and perform complex actions in dynamic environments. In contrast, planning in many simple environments is nearly intractable for robots. The goal of my research is to answer questions: "what is the purpose of this machine" and "how will it interact with humans?"

Planning with Sequence Model



Graphical overview of the planner
Algorithm sketch:
  1. Take a robotic planner, RRT*.
  2. Sample an intermediate point.
  3. Steer to that point along a high-likelihood path according to the sequence model λ.
  4. Choose the optimal path according to λ.
