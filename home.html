Hi, welcome to Yen-Ling's website!

I'm now a software engineer in Google Mountain View, working on shopping ads features.

Before joining Google, I was a master student in Intelligent Agents Lab at National Taiwan University and a visiting student in Software Agent Group at MIT Media Lab. My research focuses on crowdsourcing, reasoning, and integration of commonsense knowledge for intelligent agents and user interfaces. My master thesis is the best summarization of the work.

My interests span the fields of context-aware & multi-agent systems, commonsense reasoning, human computation, storytelling, and intelligent agents. The questions I'm currently interested in are:
  • How to build agent that understands context and use it to facilitate human decision making?
  • How to coordinate machines and have them collaborate with human to solve real world problems?
  • How to transform machine-learned statistical models to symbolic programming and make an environment programmable?
