2008 Nightmarket Workshop at Tainan

Nightmarket workshop is the first event I participated that brings people with art, design, and technology background together. In the 5-day workshop, I worked with Marisa Jahn and Stephanie Rothenberg, and other teammate (in the photo) to create participatory art installation to bring up awareness of food safety and genetic modified food to general public.

The idea of this installation: In many places, people did not pay much attention to the source of food and the problems of genetic modified food when they make purchase decision. In this work, we "invent" new fruits like zebra orange and monkey kiwi and sold in nightmarkets at Tainan to bring up people's attention to this issue.
